Pizza Recipe

Description

Why travel to Italy when you can bring Italy to your kitchen? Our ultimate pizza recipe is here to make your taste buds sing “That’s Amore!”

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Souce
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Make the sauce.
  3. Roll the dough onto a pan.
  4. Add toppings to dough.
  5. Cook pizza in the oven.
