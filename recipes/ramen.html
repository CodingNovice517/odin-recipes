Ramen Recipe

Description

Craving something warm and comforting? Dive into our sensational ramen recipe and let the noodle-nirvana begin!

Ingredients

  • Chicken broth
  • Water
  • Soy sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Miso paste
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ginger, sliced
  • Noodles
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine chicken broth, water, soy sauce, miso paste, and garlic.
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes. Strain broth to remove garlic and ginger slices.
  3. Cook the noodles.
  4. Prepare the toppings.
  5. Assemble the ramen.
