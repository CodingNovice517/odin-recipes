Lasagna Recipe

Description

Ready to take your taste buds on a joyride to flavor town? Look no further! Our ultimate lasagna recipe is here to sweep you off your feet, one luscious layer at a time.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
